## What this is

VERDICT WEIGHT&trade; is a confidence scoring framework that lets autonomous AI systems quantify, audit, and defend the trustworthiness of their own decisions. It composes eight independent evidence streams into a single, calibrated confidence score &mdash; with cryptographic provenance and a registry-level kill switch for adversarial conditions.

It is built for environments where AI decisions carry real consequence: defense, critical infrastructure, regulated industry, and any deployment where "the model said so" is not an acceptable audit trail.
